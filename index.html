WALKWRAP™

Patent-Pending Ergonomic Pallet Wrapping Tool • Invented by Cornelius Aurelius (Samuel Price)

The First Affordable Upright Pallet Wrapping Tool

WALKWRAP™ is a patent-pending manual pallet wrapping device that allows any operator to wrap pallets while standing upright, without bending or crouching. Designed for warehouses, logistics, and packaging environments, WALKWRAP™ reduces injury, increases speed, and removes the need for expensive wrapping machines.

Key Features

  • Upright ergonomic handle with adjustable height
  • Dual 360° swivel wheels for smooth walking motion
  • Low film roll mount for full bottom wrapping with no bending
  • Quick-swap film holder with tension control
  • Spiral film catcher for instant reuse
  • Dual-mode: floor-rolling and handheld for top layers
  • Lightweight aluminium / reinforced polymer frame

Why WALKWRAP™ Matters

Manual pallet wrapping causes repeated bending and twisting, leading to fatigue and avoidable workplace injuries. Full wrapping machines cost £2,000–£15,000, making them inaccessible to many warehouses.

WALKWRAP™ solves this by providing a low-cost, ergonomic, portable alternative that improves speed by 30–40% and eliminates back strain — all while being simple to manufacture.
